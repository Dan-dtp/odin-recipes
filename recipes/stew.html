Chicken Stew

Description

Stew with chicken

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Maggi
  • Seasonings (to taste)
  • Boiled/Fried/Roasted/Grilled Chicken
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Blend the tomatoes, peppers and onions till a smooth consistency
  2. Pour into a pot
  3. Add vegetable oil
  4. Add salt, maggi and seasonings of choice
  5. Fry until there is no oil in the pot
  6. Add as much or as little water depending on quantity of stew
  7. Add chicken into stew and stir
