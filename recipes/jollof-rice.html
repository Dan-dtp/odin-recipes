Jollof Rice

Description

A West African rice dish. The dish is typically made with long-grain rice, tomatoes, onions, spices, vegetables and meat in a single pot, although its ingredients and preparation methods vary across different regions.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Bay leaves
  • Water
  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Seasonings(season to taste)
  • Vegetables (optional)
  • Tomato paste

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers and onions till it reaches a smooth consistency
  2. Pour your blended tomatoes and peppers into the pot
  3. Pour in tomato paste
  4. Add oil and leave to fry
  5. Add seasonings and salt and mix
  6. Wash rice while pepper fries
  7. After pepper has finished frying, add water relative to quantity of rice
  8. Pour the rice into the pot and leave to cook
  9. Stir periodically
  10. When almost done cooking, add vegetables and mix and leavge for a few minutes
  11. When done cooking, admire your creation
  12. Jollof rice is now ready
